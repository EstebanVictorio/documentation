Variable names *can* include:

- Uppercase letters
- Numbers
- Underscores

Variable names *cannot* include:

- Special characters (other than underscores)
- Lowercase letters
- Commas
- Reserved words. Reserved words include:

    - PORT
    - K_SERVICE
    - K_REVISION
    - K_CONFIGURATION
    - CLOUD_RUN_JOB
    - CLOUD_RUN_EXECUTION
    - CLOUD_RUN_TASK_INDEX
    - CLOUD_RUN_TASK_ATTEMPT
    - CLOUD_RUN_TASK_COUNT
    - PANTHEON_*
